OUR BLOG

Bharpur Singh Gill’, Our Newest Employee, in His Own Words

20 Apr 2020

General

In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, Khalsa Aid International has been working around the clock on the emergency food appeal. We have been creating food packages for those most vulnerable in the community and unable to leave their homes. We would like to express our gratitude to Circuit for granting us access to their fantastic route planning services. Our delivery drops are carried out by our amazing volunteers o n the frontline. Through using Circuit, they have been able to achieve efficiency by selecting the most efficient route to deliver in a time-sensitive manner. The service has also ensured extremely accurate ETAs, which are invaluable when trying to reach as many vulnerable people as possible during the day. Working with a navigation of their own choice, our volunteers are continuing to deliver efficiently
